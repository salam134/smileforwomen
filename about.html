ABOUT US

AT SMILE FOR WOMEN & CHILDREN ORGANISATION, WE ARE DEDICATED TO ENDING THE CYCLE OF VULNERABILITY FOR WOMEN AND CHILDREN. EMPOWERING WOMEN, PROTECTING CHILDREN, AND CHANGING FUTURES futures IS OUR GOAL.

OUR MISSION

TO PROVIDE IMMEDIATE SAFETY, NUTRITION AND EDUCATION TO AT-RISK WOMEN AND CHILDREN.

WHILE EQUIPING THEM WITH THE TOOLS TO BREAK THE CYCLE OF POVERTY. KINDNESS HAS NO BOUNDARIES, BECAUSE EVERY LIFE MATTERS.

OUR VISION

A FUTURE WHERE EVERY WOMAN AND CHILD LIVES WITH DIGNITY, FREE FROM VIOLENCE, FEAR AND POVERTY.

EMPOWERED TO BUILD A LIFE OF OPPORTUNITY AND HOPE.
